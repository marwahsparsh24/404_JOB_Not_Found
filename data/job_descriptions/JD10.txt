Job Summary:

We are seeking a Data Analyst (known internally as a Director of Administration) to join our team, focusing on leveraging data to optimize operations and improve decision-making within our F&B, wellness, and event sectors. 

Day-to-Day:

Data Gathering and Cleaning
Collect data from various sources, including internal databases, APIs, surveys, and web analytics.
Clean and prepare datasets, addressing missing values, outliers, and inconsistencies to ensure data integrity.
Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
Identify patterns, trends, and relationships using statistical methods like descriptive statistics, correlation analysis, and hypothesis testing
Data Modeling and Visualization
Develop data models and create visualizations (charts, graphs, dashboards) to convey insights effectively to stakeholders
Use tools like Tableau or Power BI to build intuitive dashboards for real-time monitoring.
Reporting and Presentation
Generate comprehensive reports that summarize findings, key metrics, and actionable recommendations.
Present insights and recommendations to senior management and cross-functional teams.
Collaboration
Work closely with F&B, wellness, and event teams to understand their needs and align analysis with strategic goals.
Partner with stakeholders to refine data requirements and ensure actionable outcomes.
Advanced Analytics
Apply predictive modeling, machine learning and other advanced techniques to forecast trends and identify opportunities

• Critical thinking and problem-solving abilities.

• Effective collaboration with diverse teams and stakeholders.
